My Travel Destinations

California

I don't remember very much about this trip to California since I was in elementary school at the time. I remember that we visited Disney World which I recall being a nightmare for my parents since I hated everything there. But I look back on it fondly today. We also visited my aunt and uncle who lived in the suburbs of LA. We celebrated my baby sister's birthday there and my aunt's father said something I'll never forget "When I was a little girl," to this day I have no idea what he was referring to.

We also stayed at Disney Resorts while we were in California. From what I remember it was very nice and we had a connected hotel with a different pair of aunts and uncles. Now that I think about it I might be blending two trips in my memory. Oh well. The pool was really cool but my cousin and I got yelled at for going down the waterslide at the same time.

Proxima Centauri B

Proxima Centauri B is an exoplanet orbitting the Alpha Centauri system. Alpha Centauri is the nearest star system to our own. Just 4.2 lightyears away, it would take us over 100,000 years to reach at the fastest speed ever reached by humans, ~40,000 km/h. Though technology today is quite primitive at interstellar travel, advances in technology are sure to break this top speed sometime in the near future. Not much is known about Proxima Centauri B, but we do know it is a near earth size planet orbitting the triple sun of Alpha Centauri A through B. It is possible there are aliens inhabitting this planet.

This isn't somewhere I've been per se but it is somewhere I see myself travelling to in the near future. As in immortal being I'll eventually be everywhere humans dream of one day reaching, distant planets being no exception. In fact I think I'll go there now.
