My Multi-Thousand Year Trip to Proxima Centauri B

12/12/9346 7:30 PM

Touch down this millenium on Proxima Centauri B. My capsule after thousands of years flying through the inky blackness of interstellar space has finally landed on Proxima Centauri B! Turns out I was right and there definitely is alien life on Proxima Centauri B, or at least there was. There are hundreds of structures at my crash site but not a speck of life in sight! Turns out the creative works of Liu Cixin turned out to be mostly accurate. I found the pyramids and the pendulum although it now lays on it's side. Many of the dehydratories remain, you would think they would have at least a few inhabitants remaining but I scoured every one I could find, even excavated a few but all the rolled up Tri-solarans have been reduced to ash. Oh well. I sent out a distress signal to Earth a few millenium ago, told quite a few people to listen for it, but my home planet might be abandoned for good this time. Guess I'll just sit here for a few billion more years.
