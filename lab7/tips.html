Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Bring Lots of Food

    Everyboyd likes to travel, but when travelling as a picky eater can be tough. That's why rather than breaching your comfort zone just bring a suitcase or two full of food for the trip. If you're travelling by plane it might be challenging to bring everything you need. But seafarers will have an easier time with this. When I travel by boat I like to store my chicken nuggets salted by the barrel. This will ensure I won't have to eat anything good for the entire trip!

  2. Pack Your Laptop

    Some people travel to see new places, learn new things, and meet new people. Not me! If you are anything like me you travel simply because it feels like a social necessity. Make sure to bring your laptop so you don't get bored in your hotel room for the next week. I would bring a gaming laptop so you can play any games you want on the hotel wifi, and make sure to download plenty of movies and tv shows because the connection might be spotty!

  3. Don't Talk to Strangers

    Everybody knows that in areas with a lot of tourism come a lot of strangers. In an effort to not get ripped off and to avoid any kind of social discomfort, don't talk to anyone who hasn't been thoroughly vetted. This means looking them up online or knowing them from someone else. Spontaneity is the soul of adventure but it's also the theif of your wallet. If someone looks approachable be wary!

  4. Don't Take My Advice

    All the advice above is really bad! In fact seak to do the opposite of my previous recommendations. Enjoy your trip, make it exciting, but don't forget to pack your toothbrush!
